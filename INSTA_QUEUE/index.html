About InstaQueue

InstaQueue is a lightweight campus queue and appointment management system. Students can register, book appointments with campus departments, and receive a sequential queue number. Admins can view and serve queues, update appointment status, and monitor simple analytics.

Core services include Registrar, Finance, ICT Support, Library Services, Health Unit, Student Affairs, Examination Office, and Admissions.
